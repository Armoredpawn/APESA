Cultural, Religious, and Ethnic Distribution and Tension

Unit Overview

This unit will explore the various, unique ethnicities of southern Africa, as well as how they interact with one another to create a diverse, complex region. We will cover ethnic and lingual, taboos, mores, and folkways. We will also cover the tensions between the widespread ethnicities in Southern Africa, studying the Gukurahundi massacres. On top of this, we will have another case study on the cultural consequences of the apartheid on the region as a whole.

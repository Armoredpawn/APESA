AP Exploration of Southern Africa





This course covers the following topics:

  • Historical significance of the region
  • Climates
  • Population Trends
  • Political, Cultural, and Economic Processes
  • Agriculture
  • Levels of development

Students of AP Exploration of Southern Africa (APESA) will be met with a full, in-depth class on everything about the region of Southern Africa. Oftentimes, this region is overlooked or disregarded, but with this class, the wonders, culture, history, agriculture, and much more will be revealed to everyone, changing your view on not just southern Africa but even the entire world.
